Onryō

Onryō tend to be vengeful spirits, found in Japan. Their rage often stems from betrayal or murder, and they manifest to wreak havoc on the lives of those who wronged them. They are often descibed as ghastly women, wearing the clothes they were buried in.

Drekavac

The Drekavac is a spirit of an unbaptized child, or a lost soul, and is known to haunt graveyards and abandoned villages in the Balkans. Drekavac often appear either dog-like or child-like in appearance.
